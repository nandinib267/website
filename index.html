Nandini Bhattacharya

Hi! I'm Nandini, a third-year undergrad at Ashoka University studying Psychology with a minor in Computer Science. I am an aspiring researcher of internalising disorders and I'm especially interested in the link between emotion regulation and memory processes.

In fact, I'm currently working on a thesis investigating emotional regualtion patterns in people who grew up exposed to violence using ECG and Eye Tracking technology. In the future I would like to further explore the link between emotions, memory, and stress- perhaps even investigate emotion-based interventions for disorders caused by acute stress. I will be applying to postgrad opportunities soon and have my eyes on a PhD in the future (fingers crossed!).

Outside of academics, I have recently started a small home garden with help from my mother. I am a big fan of the performing arts- theatre, music, dance- and an avid consumer (+ occasional producer) of musical theatre. I love reading and I occassionally write satire. I also cook lots! Find some pictures of all this and more on the 'Personality' section of this website :)
